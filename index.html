What is FR Legends?

FR Legends is all about drifting! From driving legendary FR (front-engine, rear-wheel-drive) drift cars at world's most iconic circuits, to customizing everything on your car including engine swaps and wide-body kits.

For the first time ever, a mobile that lets you have tandem drift battles with AI drivers, unique scoring systems based on real world competition judging rules.
